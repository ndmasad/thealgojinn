✅ The short version: We collect minimal data, we do not sell your information to anyone, and we use what little we collect only to make TheAlgoJinn better for you.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TheAlgoJinn ("we", "us", "our"), operating at thealgojinn.com, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

By using TheAlgoJinn, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

TheAlgoJinn is an educational platform providing free content about Indian stock markets. We are not a financial services company, bank, or SEBI-registered entity. We do not offer investment advice or handle any financial transactions on your behalf.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

A. Information you provide voluntarily

  • Newsletter subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is entirely optional.
  • Contact messages: If you contact us via email or social media, we retain that correspondence to respond to you.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked — collected via Google Analytics (see Section 4).
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen size — collected to ensure the site displays correctly.
  • IP address: Collected automatically by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) for security and performance purposes.
  • Referral source: How you arrived at our website (e.g., Google search, direct link, social media).

We do not collect your name, phone number, financial information, Aadhaar, PAN, or any sensitive personal information unless you explicitly provide it when contacting us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes only:

  • Newsletter delivery: To send you new articles, lessons and updates if you have subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Site improvement: To understand which content is most useful and how to improve the site experience.
  • Technical maintenance: To identify and fix errors, ensure security, and maintain site performance.
  • Communication: To respond to messages or queries you send us.

We do not use your information for advertising targeting, profiling, or selling to third parties — ever.

4. Cookies & Tracking

TheAlgoJinn uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

Essential cookies — Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data about pages visited, session duration and traffic sources. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

  • You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • We have enabled IP anonymisation in Google Analytics — your full IP address is never stored by Google Analytics.

TradingView widgets — Our live market ticker and chart widgets are powered by TradingView. TradingView may set its own cookies when these widgets load. Please refer to TradingView's Privacy Policy for details.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how some features of the site work.

5. Third-Party Services

TheAlgoJinn uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Cloudflare — Website hosting, CDN and security. Cloudflare processes traffic data to protect the site from attacks. Cloudflare Privacy Policy →
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics. Anonymised usage data is sent to Google's servers. Google Privacy Policy →
  • TradingView — Live market data widgets (ticker, charts). TradingView processes market data requests. TradingView Privacy Policy →
  • Google Fonts — Font files are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log this request. Google Privacy Policy →

We do not use advertising networks, affiliate tracking pixels, or social media tracking scripts on our website.

6. Data Storage & Security

TheAlgoJinn is a static website hosted on Cloudflare Pages. We do not operate our own database or server infrastructure that stores personal data.

  • Newsletter emails are stored with our email service provider in encrypted form.
  • Analytics data is stored with Google Analytics on Google's servers.
  • Contact emails are stored in our email inbox and are not shared with third parties.

All data transmission between your browser and TheAlgoJinn is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS provided by Cloudflare.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data. We will comply unless we are required to retain it by law.
  • Right to unsubscribe: Every newsletter email contains an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time and we will remove your email promptly.
  • Right to correction: If any information we hold about you is inaccurate, you can ask us to correct it.
  • Right to object: You can object to our processing of your data for analytics purposes at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@thealgojinn.com. We will respond within 30 days.

📧 To unsubscribe from our newsletter, simply click the unsubscribe link in any email we send you. You can also email us at hello@thealgojinn.com and we will remove you immediately.

8. Children's Privacy

TheAlgoJinn is intended for adults and is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@thealgojinn.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. External Links

TheAlgoJinn may contain links to external websites such as NSE, BSE, SEBI, Screener.in, Zerodha, Groww and other financial platforms.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. When you click a link and leave TheAlgoJinn, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify newsletter subscribers. Continued use of TheAlgoJinn after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
